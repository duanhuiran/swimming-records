Swimming World Records through History

A story about speeds

Swimming is not only a race between swimmers. It is challenge against the clock that, often, is just decided by milliseconds.

This project is an exploration of how much swimming has improved since the beginning of the XX Century. It includes two different visualizations: one that focuses in the average speeds of the world records broken from 1905 to 2016, and another one that provides information about range of times and nationality of the swimmers.
